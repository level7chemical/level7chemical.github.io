About Level 7 Chemical & Supply

Level 7 Chemical & Supply is a family owned, customer focused, premium supplier of chemicals, ingredients and supplies for all kinds of commercial, business and industrial uses. Level 7 ships a large variety of chemicals, ingredients, testing supplies, pumps, as well as other supplies, and raw materials across North America direct to entrepreneurs, small and medium-sized businesses, large corporations, and procurement specialists from the private and public sectors.   

 We provide a great selection of chemicals, plus many other supplies shipping and specialty scales, chemical transfer pumps by GoatThroat, drum and material handling equipment, and drum warmers and heaters, top testing kits and strips for chlorine levels and for pH testing, great hard surface and floor cleaners, and sanitary disinfecting Quats. 

Our goal is to help customers and provide an easy and convenient online supply experience for sourcing your chemicals, ingredients, cleaning solutions and supplies. We will be happy to help source your requirements. Even if you are looking for a chemical or supply you do not see on our website you can use our REQUEST QUOTE button and we will do whatever we can to help source what you need. Or send us an email.  Our customer service email is [email protected] We are committed to adding chemicals, ingredients, and specialty products to our product line to help our customers minimize vendors and increase availability, so contact us today. 

Level 7 Chemical wants to help you find what you are looking for and get it to you on time and safely. We have over 50 years of combined experience supplying all kinds of raw material chemicals, food ingredients and additives, specialty compounding products like chelates and surfactants, as well as cleaners, and commercial supplies and equipment like pumps and drum heaters and pH strips and testing kits. 

Our owner, Mark Hughey worked for the world's largest chemical distributor, as well as the largest regional chemical distributors around the US. You can put his, and his staff's experience to work for you. We ship all over the US, and as we expand we will be offering greater availability of products, and ship to addresses outside the continental US too.

Would you like a custom quote, or more information about what we  can help you source? Contact us today  for a custom quote or more information about your important chemical, ingredients, or supplies.
