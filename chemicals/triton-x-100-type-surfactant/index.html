TRITON X 100 type Surfactant 

TRITON™ X-100 Surfactant is widely used nonionic surfactant often used as an effective detergent, emulsifier, and dispersing.

This surfactant is commonly used in household and industrial cleaners, paints and coatings, and pulp and paper processing.

Used in many applications:

  • Agrochemicals
  • Household, commerical and industrial cleaners.
  • Paints & coatings
  • Pulp & paper
  • Textile chemicals
  • Metalworking fluids

Synonyms: 9.5 Mol Ethoxylated Octylphenol, 9.5 Mol Octylphenol Ethoxylate, Octoxynol 10, POE _9.5_ Octylphenol, Polyoxyethylene _9.5_ Octylphenol, TDET 09

Weight Per Gallon: 8.3370 lbs per gal

Boiling Point Min: 392°F (200.0°C)
HLB Value: 13.4
Cloud Point: 150.8°F (66.0°C)
Freeze Point: 43°F (6.1°C)
Pour Point: 33.8°F (1.0°C)
Non HAP
Foam Characteristics: Moderate Foam
Comments: Color, APHA 100,Viscosity @ 77 F (25 C) 240 cps

CAS: 9036-19-5
