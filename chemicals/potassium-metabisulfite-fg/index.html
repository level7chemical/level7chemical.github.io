Potassium Metabisulfite FG

Chemically very similar to Sodium Metabisulfite, Potassium Metabisulfite will not increase the sodium content of the food or wine that's it's added to. It is also a little bit stronger than Sodium Metabisulfite. It is a food preservative as well as an antioxidant so it is used frequently in wine-making. Solutions made with Potassium Metabisulfite exert a reducing and bleaching action.

Typical Uses:

  • Antioxidant
  • Antiseptic
  • Bleaching Agent
  • Lithography
  • Photographic
  • Preservative
  • Reducing Agent
  • Wine

Synonyms: Dipotassium Disulphite, E 224, PBMS, Potassium Pyrosulfite

Appearance: White to Pale Yellow Fine Crystalline Powder

CAS: 16731-55-8
