Shipping

We primarily ship to commercial and industrial addresses in  the continental US within 3 to 5 days after an order is placed. Depending on your ship-to location the shipping time may add another 2 to 5 days.

We ship from  zip 72034, Conway Arkansas.  Here  is a FEDEX Priority Freight map to show shipping times using that service. 

                                            

 

There are restrictions on some products, and some products cannot be shipped to residential addresses. We currently do not ship to addresses outside the continental US, or any international destinations.

When you place an order, we will estimate shipping and delivery dates for you through the information in our Level 7 Chemical website shopping cart and the shipping options you chose. Our products primarily ship on LTL carriers / freight lines like FEDEX freight, SAIA, or XPO. 

Please also note that the shipping rates for items we sell are weight-based. The  net weight of any such item can typically be found on its detail page. Packaging, like drums and pallets add some additional weight that may not be listed on the product pages.  To reflect the policies of the shipping companies we use, the additional weight is added for pallets and drums and other needed packaging to meet the standards required by our carriers.  

If there are errors in the cart or shipping programs,  we will contact you about the problem and get you the correct information. If you are not happy with the shipping price or method you may cancel your order with no penalties or charges. You are under no obligation to complete the purchase if we have to contact you about additional charges for shipping. 

Returns Policy

Due to the nature of the chemicals and ingredients we offer, we typically do not accept returns. All returns, will be decided on a case by case basis and all returns must be approved. We typically only accept returns due to shipping the incorrect item.  If you need to return an item, please Contact Us with your order number and details about the product you would like to return. We will respond with instructions for how to return items from your order if we approve the return. 

If we do accept the return and it was not our error there may be up to a 20% restocking fee and the return  shipping may be charged to you. If approved, you should expect to receive your refund within 5 to 10 business days after the return is received by us and depending on how you paid for your order.
